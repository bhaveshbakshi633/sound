chat that travels as sound

Your message is turned into tones between 18 and 20 kHz, played out of this device's speaker, and picked up by any device listening nearby. Nothing is sent over the network. Once this page has loaded, the only thing carrying your words is air.

Open it on a second device in the same room and the same room name to see it work. On one device you'll hear your own message come back to you.

Turn the volume up. It only works out loud.
Most adults can't hear 19 kHz — but children and dogs can, and it is not silent.
